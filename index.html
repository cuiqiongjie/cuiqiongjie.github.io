Research Interests

  • Deep Learning, i.e., uses deep learning for human motion prediction our recovery
  • Deep Graph Representation, i.e., learns a pair-wise relationship for node classification
  • Machine Learning, i.e., considers the local or global consistency using low-rank or sparsity representation for human action modeling.
  • Computer Vision, i.e., gains high-level understanding from digital images, videos or other modalities.
  • Human Motion, i.e., including, human behavior understanding, action analysis, motion prediction, motion recovery, motion segmentation, etc
  • Motion Capture Technology, i.e., provides accurate human motion acquisition solution, motion capture data reuse, multimedia.
